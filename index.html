About Us

The Liberal Democratic Party of India (LDPI) was founded in March 2024 in Chandigarh, India. We strive to promote liberal values, economic liberalism, cultural progressivism, and secularism in India. Our vision is to create an inclusive society that embraces globalization and modernity while respecting our rich cultural heritage.

Policies

  • Economic Policies

    We advocate for a free-market economy that promotes foreign direct investment (FDI) and entrepreneurship. Our goal is to enhance India's competitiveness in the global market while ensuring sustainable development.

  • Social Policies

    Our social policies focus on equality, inclusivity, and respect for individual rights. We support limited reservations for underprivileged sections while encouraging meritocracy.

  • Cultural Policies

    We believe in embracing cultural diversity and promoting cultural exchanges. Our initiatives aim to introduce Western cultural elements, such as prom in schools, to foster modern values.

  • Foreign Policy

    LDPI supports strong international alliances that prioritize India's interests. We advocate for a balanced approach in global conflicts, promoting democracy and secularism.

LDPI's First National Conference

We held our first national conference on August 15, 2024, focusing on key policy initiatives and future directions for the party. The conference was attended by party members from across the country.

New Initiatives for Youth Engagement

In September 2024, LDPI launched new programs aimed at engaging youth in politics and civic activities, encouraging them to take an active role in shaping the future of India.
